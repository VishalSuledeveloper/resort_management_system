Dive Site

  • Gale Reef

    This is a dive site best accessible from Colombo and Mount Lavina. Gale Reef South is flat ground site with rows and rows of Gorgonian Fans

    Strong currents is expected. And rapid head down descent may required. Our dive master does not use anchors on this site. Redtooth Triggerfish are spotted.

  • Dawson’s Reef

    The main pass to enter the lagoon, located in its north-western end, is known as Passe Garuae and it is the largest pass in French Polynesia; the southern pass is called Tumakohua

    It has a land area of 24.1 square kilometres (9 square miles). Fakarava has 837 inhabitants; the main village is called Rotoava.

  • Claire’s REEF

    Darwin Island is the remains of an extinct volcano that reaches 165 metres (541 ft) above sea level. It is situated north-west of the main Galápagos Island group on the Wolf-Darwin Lineament

    The formation of Darwin Island is different from the formation of the main Galápagos Islands. There are currently two theories on the formation of the lineament
